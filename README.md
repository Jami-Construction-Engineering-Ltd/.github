## 🏗️ Who We Are

**Jami Construction & Engineering Ltd.** is a trusted civil and structural engineering firm based in **Dhaka, Bangladesh**. We deliver end-to-end construction solutions — from site analysis and architectural design to full-scale project execution — with an unwavering commitment to quality, safety, and on-time delivery.

> *"We don't just build structures. We build trust."*
> — **Jahangir Alam**, CEO & Founder

---

## ⚙️ Our Core Services

| Service | Description |
